Burn calories & play squash!

Indoor racquet sport



Squash is a sport played around the world and has as big a following professionally as it does in amateur ranks. The sport dates back to the 19th century with variations of the game (then called racquets) evolving into the modern game as we now know it. While squash is currently not an Olympic sport, its pinnacle comes in the form of the Squash World Championships where the best players from around the world compete to be the ultimate squash champion.
